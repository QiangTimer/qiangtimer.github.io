🌱 This series follows network probing work in three aspects:
🔭 Discover exposure: what a device or target class exposes on the network and what is worth a closer look.
🔭 Fingerprint and profile: protocols, services, and patterns that narrow the next step.
🔭 Validate the signal: cross-check probing results with firmware, config, or ground truth when possible.


I would like provide interesting findings about network probing to the community.
